# Welcome to Growth Ops Platform

The **Growth Ops Platform** is your central hub for managing DormWay's growth initiatives, campus outreach, and Labs program. This suite of tools lives inside the dormway-admin dashboard and is designed specifically for the growth team.

## Who This Is For

This documentation is for **Alex** (Campus Ambassador Recruitment & Direct Outreach Lead) and **Lauren** (Labs Program Manager & Lifecycle Lead) who use these tools daily to:

- Track and optimize **WFAU** (Weekly First-Action Users)
- Manage campus ambassador recruitment and outreach campaigns
- Coordinate Labs program tasks and deliverables
- Monitor growth metrics and performance by source

---

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Platform

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Admin Dashboard">
    Navigate to `https://admin.dormway.app` and sign in with your DormWay account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Access">
    Your account should have the `growth_ops` role - if you see a "Growth" section in the sidebar, you're all set!
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the Tools">
    Click through the Growth section in the sidebar to see all available tools
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **First time?** Start with the [Growth Scoreboard](/docs/growth-ops/scoreboard) to see your weekly metrics, then explore the other tools as needed for your workflows.
</Tip>

## Common Workflows

### Weekly Scoreboard Update (Both)

Every **Friday**, you'll update the master scoreboard with the week's metrics:

1. Open [Growth Scoreboard](/docs/growth-ops/scoreboard) in dormway-admin
2. Review **WFAU** (Weekly First-Action Users) by source
3. Check **Day 7 return %** to see retention
4. Compare performance across campuses and acquisition channels
5. Export key numbers to the team's shared scoreboard

**Goal:** Track progress toward growth targets and identify high-performing sources.

  <Accordion title="Scout and Recruit Ambassadors" icon="users">

    4. Monitor campaign performance in scoreboard
    5. Update campaign status as you progress

  <Accordion title="Process Ambassador Payouts" icon="dollar-sign">
    **Monthly task: Calculate and submit payouts**

    1. Review ambassador performance (schedule uploads tracked automatically)
    2. Calculate payouts: $5 per successful schedule upload referral
    3. Submit payout request to Riley/Ethan

## Key Metrics You'll Track

| Metric | What It Means | Where to Find It |
|--------|---------------|------------------|
| **WFAU** | Weekly First-Action Users (completed onboarding) | Growth Scoreboard |
| **Day 7 Return %** | % of users who return within 7 days | Growth Scoreboard |
| **Active Ambassadors** | Number of ambassadors with recent referrals | Campus Outreach CRM |
| **Campus Coverage** | Number of campuses with active ambassadors | Campus Outreach CRM |
| **Labs Tasks/Week** | Tasks assigned and completed | Labs Dashboard |
| **Syllabi Gathered** | Number of syllabi collected by Labs members | Labs Dashboard |

<Info>
  **WFAU is your North Star metric** - everything you do should ultimately drive more weekly first-action users. Use the other metrics to understand which channels and tactics are working best.
</Info>
