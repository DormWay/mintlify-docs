> 
> Saw your content on [specific video/topic] and thought your audience deals with the same stuff. Would love to give you early access and hear what you think. No strings attached - I'm just a student who made a thing.
> 
> [Link to demo]

**Rules:**

- Lead with "I'm a student"
- Reference their specific content
- Offer early access, not money
- Keep it casual
- One message at a time
- Follow up once after 4-5 days
