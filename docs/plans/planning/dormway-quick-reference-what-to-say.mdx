## Tone Checklist (Before Publishing)

Ask yourself:
- [ ] Would an upperclassman say this to a freshman?
- [ ] Is it specific (not vague)?
- [ ] Does it address real pain?
- [ ] Is it honest (not salesy)?
- [ ] Would I roll my eyes at this?

**If you'd roll your eyes, rewrite it.**
