## Work Plan Template

When creating new work plans, include:
1. **Objective**: What we're building and why
2. **Scope**: What's in/out of scope
3. **Timeline**: Estimated duration and milestones
4. **Resources**: Team members and dependencies
5. **Success Criteria**: How we'll know it's done
6. **Risks**: Potential blockers and mitigation

## Maintenance
- **Owner**: Engineering Leadership
- **Last Updated**: 2025-11-16
- **Review Frequency**: Weekly for active plans, monthly for backlog
- **Archival Policy**: Move completed plans to Archive after 30 days
