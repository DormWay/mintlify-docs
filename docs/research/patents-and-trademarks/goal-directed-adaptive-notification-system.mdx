## Detailed Description

### 1. Inputs

- **Engagement Goals**: defined by system or business (e.g., maximize open rates, drive purchase, increase event RSVP, reduce churn).
    
- **User Behavior Heuristics**: response history, dwell times, device usage, prior notification outcomes, sentiment preferences, tone receptivity.
    
- **Contextual Data**: time of day, location, current activity or schedule (from context engine).
    
- **Channel Metadata**: availability and constraints of push, email, SMS, in-app messaging.
    

### 2. Processing

- **Heuristic Extraction Module**: converts engagement logs into features (e.g., â€œresponds to emojis,â€ â€œprefers formal language,â€ â€œhigh open probability at 8â€“10 AMâ€).
    
- **Goal-Set LLM**: a fine-tuned model conditioned on both engagement goals and heuristics. Capable of generating multiple candidate messages optimized for different tradeoffs (click-through vs. long-term retention).
    
- **Dynamic Copy Generation**: produces notification text in real time, customized by user patterns, engagement goal, and context.
    
- **Channel & Timing Optimization**: predicts which channel and what time maximizes probability of achieving the goal. May delay delivery until a receptive moment is forecast.
    
- **Variant Generation**: creates multiple versions (A/B/C) of a notification to test and refine engagement performance in real time.
    

### 3. Output

- **Adaptive Notifications**: real-time generated, context-aware, and user-specific.
    
- **Channel Assignment**: automatically selects channel(s) and delivery window.
    
- **Goal Alignment Report**: internal metrics showing confidence and expected impact relative to the engagement goal.
    
- **Feedback Loop**: engagement outcomes update the heuristic model and retrain LLM weights or prompt strategies, creating a continuous learning cycle.
    

### 4. Embodiments

- **Mobile-only embodiment**: limited to push notifications optimized by LLMs.
    
- **Cross-channel embodiment**: includes email, SMS, and in-app banners.
    
- **Multi-LLM embodiment**: separate specialized LLMs for push vs. email vs. SMS coordinated by a central goal orchestrator.
    
- **Privacy-preserving embodiment**: all behavioral heuristics processed locally or stored in campus-partitioned databases, with only anonymized metrics used for global optimization.
