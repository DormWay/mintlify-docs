## Strategic Insight: Serve the Majority

**Key Finding:** Only 15-16% of students live in university-owned housing. However, many "off-campus" students live nearby (5-min walk) and are functionally similar to on-campus students. The real underserved groups are:
- **Distance commuters** (53% identify as commuters - likely meaning those commuting from a distance)
- **Working students** (67% work while enrolled) - need flexible, asynchronous support
- **Parents** (22% are parents) - need support that fits around family
- **First-gen students** - may not know how to navigate office hours/advising

**DormWay's Advantage:** Your asynchronous, 24/7 support (Ace LLM) is PERFECT for students who:
- Can't easily access office hours (distance commuters)
- Work during office hours (67% of students)
- Have family responsibilities (22% are parents)
- Need flexible support times

**Action:** Position DormWay as "Built for students who need flexible, 24/7 support" - this is your defensible moat!
